Careers @ Lexicon

Find out what you like doing best, and get someone to pay you for doing it.

Welcome to the Lexicon Group’s, Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence (Lexicon MILE) Job Page. If you are seeking an environment where your contribution helps people across India and worldwide make some of the most important decisions of their lives, and you want to work in a friendly environment where employees’ opinions are valued, then Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence is the place for you !

Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence belongs to the premier Sukhdev and Kamal Sharma Education Trust’s Lexicon Group of Institutes, well known all over Pune, for its chain of Lexicon CBSE and Lexicon Kids Pre-Schools boasting of a legacy of quality and value driven education, rolled out to Punekars, over the years . Founded in the year 2009, today Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence has come a long way in a short span of eight years, to be known as the most sought after B-School, in eastern Pune.

LEXICON Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence INVITES TOP CLASS ACADEMICIANS & CORPORATES ALIKE TO BE A PART OF ITS SENIOR FACULTY AND INSTITUTE BUILDING.

LEXICON’s Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence offers an AICTE Approved PGDM programme with an open culture of working, where everyone strives for making an institute par excellence. We offer best-in-class pay and perks, which includes residential benefits and a seamless bonus to cover relocation.

If you require any assistance in Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence online recruitment system, then please contact the Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence Recruitment Support team for help: careers@mile.education.
