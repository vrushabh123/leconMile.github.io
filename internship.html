INTERNSHIPS @ LEXICON

Theory without application and experience is insufficient in today’s world of education and Management Studies. To give our students adequate real-life experience and exposure, Industry Internship is a mandatory element of the PGDM Program at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence. Students are required to spend a minimum of nine months on a paid internship along with other subjects and assignments of their final year.

Internships at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence provide experience at companies in different sectors of FMCG, Banking, Insurance, Manufacturing, Consultancy, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Automobile, Telecom, IT, and innovative start-ups. These internships allow students to acquire new professional skills, apply their classroom theories and management models, and also pave the way for future full-time employment after graduation from Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence.

Industry Internship

To build the bridge between theory and practice, students are required to spend minimum nine months on a paid internship projects at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence, at the commencement of their final year of the PGDM program along with the other subjects and assignments of Trimester IV, V and VI. This experience involves corporate stints with brand companies in different sectors of FMCG, Banking, Insurance, Manufacturing, Consultancy, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Automobile, Telecom, IT etc. and interesting startups wherein students are exposed to the ground realities of a corporate life and put in a series of departments related to the management domains of Marketing, Human Resource and Finance.

Winter Internship Project

One of the USPs of the PGDM program at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence is its Winter Internship Program along with the Ministry of Forests, Pune in the form of rural research projects undertaken to understand and meet the global need to churn out socially and environmentally sensitive managers to the corporate world today. Moreover, Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence strongly believes in extending its CSR activities for the larger good of society. In order to make our budding management professionals socially and environmentally sensitive, Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence aims at a Winter Internship Project which gives our students, a life transformational experience, of being in the midst of society and nature. WIPs at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence are carried out in association with Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, the Pune Traffic Police, PMC departments and NGOs. These projects carry a total of 100 marks and are an integral part of the student’s first year course curriculum at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence. Welcome to learnings in the midst of nature and out on the streets!
