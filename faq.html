Admission FAQ’S

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the minimum educational qualification required to get into the PGDM program ?

    ANS : Graduation (minimum three year duration) in any discipline from a recognized University with mandatory minimum of 50 per cent marks. The students who are appearing for final year examination / awaiting final year results can also apply.
  2. What are the various exams considered ?

    ANS : GWe consider marks in any of the following all India examinations. CAT/ CMAT / ATMA / MAT / MH-CET / XAT/ GMAT.
  3. What are the minimum cut off scores in each of the tests that are considered ?

    ANS : Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence believes in considering the overall personality while selecting students especially with respect to their past academic records, work experience, if any, among others. There is no prescribed minimum cut off score for any test. However, due weightage is given to the test scores. For this year’s admission 20 percent weightage will be given to the test scores.
  4. Can I apply for PGDM program if I have not appeared for any of the above mentioned entrance tests?

    ANS : No.
  5. What scores do I submit if I have given all the exams ?

    ANS : You can submit all the scores of whatever you have appeared at or in wherever you have scored highest.
  6. Is the PGDM Course at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence an AICTE approved course ?

    ANS : The PGDM Course at Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence is approved by AICTE & DTE, Maharashtra, Ministry of HRD, Government of India.
  7. What is the intake of students in Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence PGDM program ?

    ANS : Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM): 120 seats.
  8. How many offices does Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence have in India ?

    ANS : For the purpose of ease of PGDM Admission operations and in order to help parents and family of students, to make the admission process more user friendly and trustworthy, apart from the Head office situated in Pune, Lexicon Management Institute of Leadership and Excellence also operates its admission processes from Indore and Kolkatta.
